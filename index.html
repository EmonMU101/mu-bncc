The Foundation & Vision

Our Mission

The Bangladesh National Cadet Corps (BNCC) is a voluntary organization that works to shape young students into disciplined, confident, and responsible citizens. Guided by the Bangladesh Army, BNCC provides leadership and military based training.

Our Values

Metropolitan University BNCC Platoon motivates students to become skilled, compassionate, and socially responsible individuals ready to serve the country.

Igniting the Movement

Our Journey

After a selection round held in May of last year, 24 cadets were officially chosen for the first batch, guided by a PUO and Army staff member.
