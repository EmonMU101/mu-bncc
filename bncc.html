The Foundation & Vision

Our Mission

The Bangladesh National Cadet Corps (BNCC) is a voluntary organization that works to shape young students into disciplined, confident, and responsible citizens. Guided by the Bangladesh Army, BNCC provides leadership and military based training that helps students develop patriotism, teamwork, dedication, and the courage to face real life challenges.

Our Values

Alongside physical and tactical training, BNCC also encourages cadets to take part in social welfare activities, disaster response programs, and community service, helping them grow both as individuals and future leaders of the nation. Metropolitan University BNCC Platoon proudly carries the spirit and values of this respected organization. Through commitment, discipline, and unity, the platoon motivates students to become skilled, compassionate, and socially responsible individuals who are always ready to serve the country and contribute positively to society.

Igniting the Movement

Our Journey

Metropolitan University BNCC Platoon began its journey with the participation of nearly a hundred students. After a selection round held in May of last year, 24 cadets were officially chosen for the first batch. At the beginning, there were no ranks among the cadets, and the platoon was guided by a PUO and Army staff member.

Growth & Development

With time, dedication, and teamwork, the platoon has grown into a unit of 30 cadets. The platoon now proudly includes one Sergeant, three Corporals, three Lance Corporals, and the remaining cadets, all working together with discipline and commitment under the guidance of a new PUO and Army staff member.

Major Participation

Our Achievements

This Platoon has proudly participated in various national programs, camps, competitions, and official duties with discipline and dedication. Our cadets successfully represented the university in:

  • GOC Guard of Honor
  • Battalion Camp Drill and Cultural Competitions
  • Debate and Chess Competitions
  • National Armed Forces Day 2025
  • Independence Day Drill
  • Victory Day Parade
  • Central Camp Band Training
  • BPL Duty, Election Duty, Prime Minister's Visit Duty
  • And many other important events

Through every participation, our cadets demonstrated teamwork, leadership, professionalism, and the true spirit of BNCC, bringing pride and recognition to Metropolitan University.

National Recognition

Excellence Recognized

Metropolitan University BNCC Platoon has earned notable recognition through its disciplined and outstanding performances at various national events. From receiving high praise during the GOC visit to winning medals and top positions in Battalion and Central Camps, including champion and runner-up titles in drill competitions, our cadets have consistently excelled.

We have also achieved national recognition in cultural segments, proving our strength in both discipline and talent. Overall, in every event we took part in, we made our presence known through excellence, discipline, and pride.
